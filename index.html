About Isan

Isan (or Isaan) is a vibrant region in northeastern Thailand known for its strong cultural identity, unique cuisine, and artisanal traditions. While less traveled than the beaches of the south or the mountains of the north, Isan offers a deep and authentic Thai experience — from silk weaving villages to ancient Khmer ruins.

Explore the Region

Visit spectacular national parks, quiet temples, and friendly towns like Ubon Ratchathani, Khon Kaen, and Nakhon Phanom. Each offers a different facet of Isan life — colorful festivals, riverfront markets, and centuries-old history.

Local Crafts & Products

Isan is home to some of Thailand’s most renowned artisans, especially in silk weaving, basketry, pottery, and natural dyeing. Our platform will soon highlight selected craftspeople and their stories — giving you access to beautiful handmade goods and the people behind them.
